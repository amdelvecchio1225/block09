Hobbies

A lot of my free time is dedicated to indulging in my hobbies, the largest of which being video games. I play video games almost every day, whether it be with friends or by myself. Video games allows me to clear my head after a stressful day, or to just have some fun after a long day of class or work. My free time is also sometimes spent learning game development. While this has been put to the side in favor of focusing on my career with Fullstack, game development has been something I've been interested in since I was little, and I started learning it during high school. With this, I picked up some coding knowledge early on, which helped give a small headstart in my computer science courses once I graduated high school and went to college.
